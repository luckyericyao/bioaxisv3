# BioAxis Product Catalog Research Notes

Internal traceability notes for the product-item catalog build. Sources were used to understand common product-detail structures, specification fields, applications, and documentation patterns. BioAxis page copy remains original, generic, and sourcing-focused.

## Pipette tips and liquid handling

Sources reviewed:
- Thermo Fisher pipette tip category pages
- Corning/Axygen filtered pipette tip pages
- Supplier pages for manual and automation liquid handling tips

Common specification fields:
- volume range
- filtered or non-filtered barrier
- sterile status
- low-retention surface
- DNase/RNase-free or PCR-clean requirement
- racked, reload, nested rack, or bulk format
- pipette or liquid-handler compatibility

Common applications:
- PCR and qPCR setup
- molecular biology sample prep
- cell culture support
- assay setup
- automated liquid handling

Documentation fields observed:
- CoA where available
- sterility statement
- nuclease-free statement
- material declaration
- lot traceability

BioAxis implementation:
- Use generic product-configuration pages by volume, filter barrier, surface, sterility, packaging, and platform-fit review.
- Phrase brand-related needs as compatibility review, not direct manufacturer equivalence.

## PCR plastics and qPCR consumables

Sources reviewed:
- Thermo Fisher PCR and qPCR plate pages
- General supplier PCR plate, tube, strip, and optical-seal category pages

Common specification fields:
- 96-well, 384-well, tube, strip, or film format
- skirted, semi-skirted, or non-skirted plate geometry
- profile height
- optical clarity
- sealing compatibility
- thermal cycler or qPCR instrument compatibility
- PCR-clean or DNase/RNase-free status

Common applications:
- PCR
- qPCR
- RT-PCR
- genotyping
- high-throughput molecular workflows

Documentation fields observed:
- nuclease-free or PCR-clean statements
- CoA where available
- lot traceability
- storage condition
- instrument-fit notes where available

BioAxis implementation:
- Product item pages ask for instrument model, seal format, optical needs, cleanliness requirement, and sample testing criteria.

## Assays, antibodies, buffers, small equipment, and single-use

Sources reviewed:
- Bio-Rad ELISA component pages
- Thermo Fisher antibody category pages and selection guides
- Sigma-Aldrich buffer and document-search pages
- Eppendorf benchtop centrifuge category pages
- Cytiva extractables/leachables information
- Sartorius and Avantor single-use fluid-management pages
- Qiagen sample-prep product pages

Common specification fields:
- assay format, plate color, signal type, and reader compatibility
- antibody target, host species, clonality, conjugation, and validation application
- reagent grade, concentration, pH, storage condition, CoA, and SDS
- equipment capacity, footprint, voltage, compatible consumables, warranty, and calibration needs
- single-use material, connector type, sterility or irradiation status, process scale, and extractables/leachables documentation

Common applications:
- ELISA and cell-based assays
- Western blot, flow cytometry, immunofluorescence, and pull-down workflows
- buffer preparation, molecular biology, and protein analysis
- sample preparation, mixing, temperature control, and basic measurement
- process development, sterile sampling, and single-use fluid handling

Documentation fields observed:
- CoA, SDS, storage condition, target/application information, operating requirements, material declarations, sterility statements, lot traceability, and process-contact documentation where relevant

BioAxis implementation:
- General product configurations cover every family with specification-led sourcing fields.
- Priority product pages add more specific configurations where buyer input commonly changes route, compatibility, or evaluation needs.
